Instructions

In this game you will take control over your very own burger shop. You can sell burgers for money. With your profit, by new toppings for higher profits or hire staff to make you money when you are idle.

Now come on, what are you waiting for? It is time to start making profit. Get ready, you're in for a ride!

Click the burgers to earn profit. When you have enough money, buy upgrades for increased profit. The more upgrades you have, the more profit you make.

Note: For best experience use full screen.
